What is an Eco-Score?

The Eco-Score is a measure of your environmental impact based on your lifestyle choices. It helps you understand how your daily activities contribute to your carbon footprint and offers insights into how you can reduce your impact on the planet.

Calculate Your Eco-Score

Tips to Improve Your Eco-Score

  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances to reduce energy consumption.
  • Take shorter showers and fix leaks to conserve water.
  • Recycle and compost to minimize waste production.
  • Use public transport, carpool, or cycle to reduce your carbon footprint.
